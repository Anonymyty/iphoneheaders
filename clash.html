About my Clash life

Hi, I'm ipad_kid. I officially retired from Clash of Clans on September 9th, 2016- over three years after I had originally started playing. From December 2015 to August 2016, I played Clash as a lifestyle. During those nine months, I spent an estimated 700 total hours on Clash. I farmed about 1.5 billion combined Gold and Elixir, and 2.5 million Dark Elixir in that time. I also donated over 600 thousand equivalent troop capacity in those nine months, taking me up to level 200, and reaching level 202 before my retirement. Right up until I retired, I was working on farming my King and Queen up to level 30. My account was a fully maxed Town Hall 9 except for Heroes. My player tag is #QPOQCJRQ. I'm also on ClashTrack where it's easy to find all the statistics about my base.

As of December 2, 2016, my Clash account has been handed over to a friend of mine who goes on every once in awhile to check on things and collect resources.
